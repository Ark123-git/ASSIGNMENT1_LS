Semester 1

Semester 1 was all about exploring the campus meeting new people and making a ton of friends.We were exposed to the culture of IIT Bombay.The late night canteens,last-minute study and sleepless nights.

Semester 2

Before entering semester 2 I had one of the best times of my insti life enjoying the cultural fest of IIT Bombay (MOOD INDIGO).The fest was an absolute blast.Semester 2 was more about exploring different competitions and hanging out.
